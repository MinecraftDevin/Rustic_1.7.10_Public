Hello!

Hello all, this is the site to currently hold my project, "Rustic". Rustic I plan to make as a steampunk themed mod. I am currently new to the whole Minecraft modding scene, so you will have to forgive any delay in updates/releases.

About

I am currently taking classes and self teaching myself Java. I started out using Lua thanks to Computercraft. I decided to move onto Java to start looking into Minecraft Modding. As I learn better skills, I plan to apply them to the mod to make it more like I vision it.

Plans

Here are some of the ideas I am going to be starting with:

  • Steam power(not steam that goes into engines but into the machines themselves)
  • Prototyping Table(5x5 crafting grid with 4 tool slots)
  • Mechanical Spider fimliar
  • Cook Stove
  • Decor Blocks, such as a Grandfather Clock
  • Steampunk themed tools and weapons
  • Custom rendered armor

These will most likely change, mainly due to the fact that I still need to learn how to make some of the items. I plan to have more then just this, like I said this is just a start.
